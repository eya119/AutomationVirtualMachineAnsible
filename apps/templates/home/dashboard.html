Our Services

  • In 2008 a restructuring of the group into a holding company took place. The latter formalizes the de facto separation between founders and shareholders as well as the day-to-day management of the business centers to ensure their sustainability. In 2010, PGH launched a restructuring action which gave rise to the recentralization of its group around 8 businesses to facilitate management and performance monitoring. The 8 professions are poultry integration, consumer products, steel processing, packaging, real estate, wood and capital goods, construction materials and trade and services. The holding company, whose main role is to ensure the competitiveness, sustainability and development of the group, manages shareholdings in the subsidiaries, implements the group's strategy and centralizes certain common activities. The basic element is the hybrid organization or double hierarchy which allows it to assist, supervise and supervise the subsidiaries and develop synergies between them.
